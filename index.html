WHY NO HYGGE
Has the concept of hygge recently entered your consciousness? You’re not alone. Hygge, or the Danish idea of “coziness,” can be found in anything from a roaring fire to a perfect pair of lumpy socks, and it has long been a vital aspect of the Scandinavian culture. And 2016 was the year in which it moved west.
